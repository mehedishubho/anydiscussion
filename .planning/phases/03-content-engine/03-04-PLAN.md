## Phase Goal

**As an** editor/admin,
**I want to** publish posts (immediately or scheduled) and share draft preview links with stakeholders,
**so that** cached pages reliably refresh on publish (Pitfall #3), scheduled posts go live at their set time without manual intervention, and reviewers can read drafts before approval.

This is **Slice D** of the Phase-3 MVP vertical-slice plan: publishing, revalidation, scheduling, and preview. It composes Slice A's posts.ts + Slice B's renderPostBody + Slice C's settings seed (site.timezone). After this slice, the Phase-3 vertical-slice set is complete: an author can write, submit for review, share a preview link, and an editor can publish (now or scheduled) with cached pages reliably refreshing.
