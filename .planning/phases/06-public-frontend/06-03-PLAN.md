Default page export PostPage({ params }) — await params, call getPostForPublic(slug). If null, call notFound(). Extract post, seo, author from the result. Render an article:
1. BlogPosting JSON-LD: a real `<script type="application/ld+json" dangerouslySetInnerHTML={{ __html: JSON.stringify(blogPostingJsonLd({...})) }} />` (per Phase 5 Pitfall 2 pattern).

3. BODY — rendered SYNCHRONOUSLY via renderPostBody(post.body) then dangerouslySetInnerHTML. NO Suspense around the body (it is the LCP — RESEARCH Anti-Patterns: wrapping it makes it stream, tanking LCP). Prose classes: className="prose prose-lg max-w-none dark:prose-invert".

| T-06-09 | Tampering | ViewCount without connection() | high | mitigate | connection() from next/server is called as the FIRST line — prevents build hangs and silent caching that would freeze the count at 1 (Pitfall 1) |
